This survey is conducted by researchers at the University of Manchester with mySociety as part of a project examining how and why citizens use websites like . The main goal is to improve the users’ experience and to help researchers understand whether offers a new way for people to become involved in their communities and in politics. The survey shouldn’t take more than 10-15 minutes to be completed.

Individual responses will be kept completely confidential. Only the aggregate data will be reported; your individual responses will never be published or identified.
